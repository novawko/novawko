AT A GLANCE

The Kitiguars are an open species created by me. They are a feline-like, furry, and noodly dragon species with some forbidden traits. They were first in development in 2020 and got revamped in 2025. Originally, they had long claws that stay out but I decided to make them more friendly and such since they're the symbol of love, so they can be pictured without claws since they can unsheathe.

The Kitiguars (and the Naa) were on my personal site aka here at first but I decided to move the species to their own site. But then after since Netlify's new free plan changed recently, the species are back onto my site. You may create your own Kitiguar without permission but it be submitted for approval via Google Forms. Thank you!

OVERVIEW

The Kitiguars live on their own islands on Earth. The islands have a connecting "bridge" where they travel to the other regions and tribes. However, the Kitiguars do get into war between regions and with tribes. During every full moon, they gather somewhere on the outskirts of Cavern Region where they discuss any disagreements and recent wars if any. The Kitiguars are the symbol of love and affection so they are trying their best to help everyone in peace and justice. Tribes don't attend the gatherings since they're considered outsiders and are smaller versions of the regions.

ANATOMY

Kitiguars have a very similar anatomical structure to that of cats. They have very flexible spines, starting from their neck/skull all the way down to the tip of their tail. Also, a Kitiguar's claws can unsheathe. Just like their skeletions, Kitiguars have a lot of similarities to cats. All of their organs work just the same as cats. They usually eat meat but also herbs, so they're omnivores. The claws are the same built and structure as cats as well. The muscles tighten when their claws are out meaning they're stressed or defensive. Kitiguars only have paws, no hooves or other types of feet. The teeth are very similar to rats. Kitiguars are commonly seen with three different types of tongues, regular, pointed, and forked.

PELT

Once more, Kitiguars have the same patterns and pelt as cats. They come in various types of pelt colors and patterns. They're normally natural-colored but it's more rarer for a bright and neon Kitiguar to be born. Kitiguars also can dye their pelt to look like they were born unnatural. This is uncommon for a Kitiguar to do.

Height

Adult Kitiguars are as big as a Siberain Husky dog (roughly 24 inches tall) or as small as an average cat (about 12 inches tall). Baby Kitiguars are as small as a baby kitten (3 to 6 inches long). Kitiguars can also be undersized or oversized. Usually, an oversized Kitiguar are highly likely to be appointed as their group's leader depending on their role. Otherwise, an undersized Kitiguar are usually ranked lower than their peers.

Locations

Kitiguars are located anywhere throughout the world of Nubreolan but are primary located between Asia and Europe. Some Kitiguars have special traits in order to survive the climate at their living space. For example, Kitiguars have long fur in the Arctic to help keep warm. While Kitiguars in the desert have very short fur to being hairless to prevent from overheating. Kitiguars love to follow human culture and get very curious and fascinated about items made by humans. Otherwise, Kitiguars generally collect rocks, shells, pearls, bones, stones, crystals and such.
