Anatomy .

Kitiguars have a very similar anatomical structure to that of cats. They have very flexible spines, starting from their neck/skull all the way down to the tip of their tail. Also, a Kitiguar's claws can unsheathe.

Just like their skeletions, Kitiguars have a lot of similarities to cats. All of their organs work just the same as cats. They usually eat meat but also herbs, so they're omnivores.

The claws are the same built and structure as cats as well. The muscles tighten when their claws are out meaning they're stressed or defensive. Kitiguars only have paws, no hooves or other types of feet.

The teeth are very similar to rats. Kitiguars are commonly seen with three different types of tongues, regular, pointed, and forked.

. Pelt

Once more, Kitiguars have the same patterns and pelt as cats. They come in various types of pelt colors and patterns. They're normally natural-colored but it's more rarer for a bright and neon Kitiguar to be born. Kitiguars also can dye their pelt to look like they were born unnatural. This is uncommon for a Kitiguar to do.

Height .

Adult Kitiguars are as big as a Siberain Husky dog (roughly 24 inches tall) or as small as an average cat (about 12 inches tall). Baby Kitiguars are as small as a baby kitten (3 to 6 inches long). Kitiguars can also be undersized or oversized. Usually, an oversized Kitiguar are highly likely to be appointed as their group's leader depending on their role. Otherwise, an undersized Kitiguar are usually ranked lower than their peers.

. Regions

Kitiguars are located anywhere throughout the world of Nubreolan but are primary located between Asia and Europe. Some Kitiguars have special traits in order to survive the climate at their living space. For example, Kitiguars have long fur in the Arctic to help keep warm. While Kitiguars in the desert have very short fur to being hairless to prevent from overheating. Kitiguars love to follow human culture and get very curious and fascinated about items made by humans. Otherwise, Kitiguars generally collect rocks, shells, pearls, bones, stones, crystals and such.

Fluffy Worms .

  • They are an open species heavily inspired by the Worm-on-a-String/Magical Worm toy.
  • They're tiny as a house mouse to being big as a rabbit.
  • Fluffy Worms have huge and wide eyes with large pupils that almost cover their eyes. Good for seeing in the dark.
  • Their pelt are generally wiry and puffy as a cloud but can be ragged or with long fur. They also come in various colors and patterns!
  • Their tongue are extremely long and sticky that acts as a butterfly while eating.
  • No legs! They move around like a snake.
  • Fluffy Worms are only aggressive when feeling in danger or protecting their mate. Otherwise, they love cuddling!

. The Naa

  • They are an open species.
  • They're small as a Syrian hamster.
  • The Naa have a feathered coat but they cannot fly.
  • They come in various colors and patterns! Think of their markings like birds or like cats.
  • They have razor sharp teeth and slit pupils!
  • They have stubby short legs like a hamster and can run fast.
  • They act very much like hamsters where a Naa of the same gender cannot be in the same room together since they will fight.
  • The Naa also also a little bit like a deer with their antlers to defend themselves while attacking or fighting.
  • They have a long rat tail or no tail. Also, their ears are round like a hamster.
  • No whiskers!

Trivia .

  • Kitiguars can be kept as pets by humans, so Kitiguars can be very protective over their owners.
  • Kitiguars can also have their own pets.
  • Most Kitiguars really adore Fluffy Worms as their companions.
