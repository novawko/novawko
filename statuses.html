What are systems?
You will notice the different tone tags that are exclusive to systems. What's a system? An OSDD (Other Specified Dissociative Disorder) system is a group of alters, formed by repeated childhood trauma from ages 1 to 12 usually. Early teenagers usually start forming OSDD, or even earlier.

Read throughout this Carrds that I've referenced!
  • osddeducation.carrd.co
  • didosdd.carrd.co
  • osdddidinfo.carrd.co
OSDD is like multiple people trapped in one body/person, like different personalities. It's a mixture of both. For me, personally, I feel like it's multiple personalities!

Keep in mind that I never really talked to a system before and I'm still learning about them. Feel free to contact if I need to update anything!
