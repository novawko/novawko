STATUSES

What's this and why?
These are commonly seen in Discord user's statuses, and I didn't know what some of them mean at first until I came across a Carrd about them.

MASTERLIST

  • SLINT — Selective Interaction
  • STI — Safe To Interact
  • OTI — Okay to Interact
  • FTI — Free to Interact
  • PIU — Please Interact
  • ATI — Ask to Interact
  • IWSC — Interact With Some Caution
  • AIWCAU — Always Interact With Caution
  • AIWC — Always Interact With Care
  • AIWEC — Always Interact With Extreme Care
  • IWC/IWCR — Interact With Care/Caution
  • IWEC/IWECR — Interact with Extreme Care/Caution
  • IWVECA — Interact with Very Extreme Caution
  • IAYOR — Interact At Your Own Risk
  • BGWI — Be Gentle When Interacting
  • BEGWI — Be Extremely Gentle When Interacting
  • DNIIC — Do Not Interact If Close
  • DNIUI — Do Not Interact Unless Interacted (before)
  • DNIUC — Do Not Interact Unless Close
  • DNIUVC — Do Not Interact Unless Very Close
  • DNIUEC — Do Not Interact Unless Extremely Close
  • DNIUCP — Do Not Interact Unless Comfort People
  • DNIUF/DNIUM — Do Not Interact Unless Friended/Mutuals
  • DNIUGP — Do Not Interact Unless Given Permission
  • DNIUP/PS — Do Not Interact Unless Partner/Partner System
  • DNIAGR, REGDNI, AGRDNI — (Age) Regressed, Do Not Interact
  • NSTI — Not Safe To Interact
  • DNIU — Do Not Interact Unless Names Listed After
  • DNIUID — Do Not Interact Unless I Do
  • DNI(/srs or /vsrs) — Do Not Interact, period. Some people may be acceptations.
  • DNIUFP — Do Not Interact Unless Favorite Person* *Only used by people with BPD
  • AFK — Away From Keyboard/Device
  • AFD — Away From Discord* *On current device but on another app with Discord in the background
  • ONL — Online* *On current device but focused/only on Discord
