PRIVACY POLICY

I ensure that personal information, such as emails, PayPal for purchases, Discord usernames, etc. are not given away to any third party sites or publicly shared anywhere. I value everyone's privacy. If I discover that there is a breach of privacy, whether intentional or non-intentional, I will take quick action to ensure your information's safety.

If you make artwork or character designs for me, you are deemed the sole owner, but I will read everyone's TOS of their designs and art to ensure that I can use off-site without a Toyhou.se account. I respect your wishes and rules if restricted. Otherwise, if in question, I will contact you in advance before posting onto my site.
